Your Child Deserves The Best
  1  Personalised & Flexible Learning

With R P Mission School your child and you get the freedom to continue in the same school regardless of your situation and location.The curriculum is also beneficial for gifted students, professional athletes and slow learners as the student can decide the pace.

  2  Track Students Progress Daily

Our tech enabled learning platform provides relevant and timely feedback so you can monitor student's progress and we can use the data to analyse the pain points and drive focus towards it, improving learning outcomes.

  3  Skill Based Learning

We cover the benchmarks of the national curriculum framework and couple that with several electives that the student can choose from during their time at R P Mission School. With students not wasting time for commute, many take up language studies, performing arts and sports during their free time.

  4 Socialise with Friends Around the World

As R P Mission is location agnostic, we select top faculty from around the country for our school this is extremely beneficial for students that do not have access to quality education in home town. With students from other cities and abroad, your friendship circle will be global.
